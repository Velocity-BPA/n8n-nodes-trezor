# Automate Your Crypto Operations: Introducing the n8n Trezor Node

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node for n8n: the Trezor Hardware Wallet integration. At Velocity BPA, we're constantly working to bridge the gap between traditional business automation and emerging technologies, and this node does exactly that for cryptocurrency operations.

## The Challenge

Managing cryptocurrency operations at scale has always been a challenge for businesses and power users. Whether you're running a crypto treasury, managing multiple wallets, or implementing privacy-focused transactions, the manual process of connecting hardware wallets to various systems is time-consuming and error-prone. Trezor devices are industry-leading hardware wallets, but integrating them into automated workflows has been notoriously difficult—until now.

## What Makes This Node Special

The n8n-nodes-trezor brings enterprise-grade crypto automation to your fingertips. This integration allows you to incorporate Trezor hardware wallet operations directly into your n8n workflows, maintaining the security of hardware-based key storage while gaining the power of automation.

Key features include:

**Multi-Chain Support**: Work seamlessly across Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other supported cryptocurrencies without switching tools or workflows.

**CoinJoin Privacy**: Implement privacy-focused transaction workflows with built-in CoinJoin support, perfect for businesses that prioritize financial privacy.

**Device Management**: Monitor and manage your Trezor devices programmatically, enabling you to build sophisticated monitoring and alerting systems.

**Secure by Design**: All operations maintain the security model of your Trezor device—private keys never leave the hardware wallet.

## Real-World Use Cases

Imagine automatically monitoring your crypto treasury and receiving alerts in Slack when balances hit certain thresholds. Or perhaps you need to batch process transactions while maintaining the security of hardware wallet signatures. With this node, these workflows become straightforward to implement.

## Getting Started

Installing the Trezor node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-trezor


After restarting n8n, you'll find the Trezor node available in your node palette, ready to be incorporated into your workflows.
